Conventional Fixed Rate loans

It is a fixed rate mortgage which comes with an interest rate. This will not change until your home loan comes to an end. This loan conforms to establish guidelines for the size of the loan and your financial situation. It can finance a primary residence, vacation home, rental property, and many more.

Federal Housing Administration Loans

A home mortgage that is insured by the government and issued by a bank or other lender that is approved by the Federal Housing Administration. It is specifically designed for people from low to moderate income groups.

  • Lower credit score requirements

  •     (% Rates and APR)

Veteran Affairs Loans

VA loans are available to active and veteran service personnel along with their surviving spouses. They are backed by the federal government. No down payment is required, and has competitively low interest rates. It offers limited closing costs and No need for private mortgage insurance. The VA loan is a lifetime benefit; you can use the guarantee multiple times.

Why Choose Us?

At US Mortgage Interest Rates, we're here to help you find the right home loan for your needs. We're committed to providing you with the highest level of service, and we'll work with you every step of the way to make sure you're comfortable with your loan.

1Is pre-closure or part payment allowed in a home loan?

Yes, pre-closure or part payment is allowed with most home loan providers. You may need to check with your lender to see if they impose any additional fees or charges for early repayment. However, you can expect to be able to make significant savings in interest payments by making pre-payments or making additional regular payments over and above the loan EMI amount.

2How much loan can I avail?

The amount of loan you can avail depends on a variety of factors such as your income, credit score, current obligations, etc. We recommend that you use our Home Loan Eligibility Calculator to get a better idea of the loan amount you may be eligible for.

3What is pre-EMI interest?

Pre-EMI interest is the interest that you pay on a Home Loan before the loan start date. This type of interest applies to any home loans taken out in advance, and is generally charged at a flat rate until the loan starts. Check your monthly EMI now!
